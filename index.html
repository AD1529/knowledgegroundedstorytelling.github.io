Knowledge-Grounded Narrative Generation through Thematic and Situational Constraints in Human–AI Co-Creation

Angelica Lo Duca and Daniela Rotelli

This paper presents a knowledge-grounded framework for human–AI narrative co-creation that treats storytelling as a constraint-aware generative process. It distinguishes theme as a global semantic constraint and subject as the concrete narrative situation, showing how layered guidance can shape coherent, meaningful stories.

Workflow

The workflow organizes co-creation into five stages:

  • Theme and subject definition.
  • Knowledge grounding with thematic and situational constraints.
  • Character construction.
  • Plot structuring.
  • Narrative generation with iterative human judgment and refinement.
