Questions

  1. Narrative coherence. Narrative coherence refers to the logical, consistent, and meaningful alignment of all elements within a story, ensuring that events, characters, and themes fit together in a unified way that is clear to the audience.

  2. Emotional engagement. Emotional engagement is the process of creating meaningful, lasting connections by evoking strong feelings in an audience, consumer, or participant through storytelling, design, or experiences.
